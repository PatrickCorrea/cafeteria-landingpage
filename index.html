About Us

Welcome to Happy Coffee!

Here, each cup is a unique experience. We select the best grains, grown sustainably by small producers, to guarantee maximum quality. Our welcoming environment is the perfect place to relax, work or enjoy good conversation. In addition to specialty coffees, we offer aromatic teas and artisanal delicacies, all prepared with care by our talented team. We value the local community and seek to support nearby producers and suppliers. Visit us and let yourself be enchanted by Café Encanto. We look forward to sharing our passion with you!
